# MUVid_Dataset
The MUVid (Munzur University-driving Labeled Video Dataset) dataset is a dataset consisting of road images recorded under real driving conditions in the campus areas of Munzur University in Turkiye. The dataset was developed for the purpose of semantic segmentation of drivable road areas under low light conditions. The dataset, consisting of a total of 307 images, was manually labeled with the CVAT labeling tool and 80% was allocated as training, 10% as validation, and 10% as test set.
